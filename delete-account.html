Gotcha • Account & Data Deletion

Request deletion of your Gotcha account and data

This page explains how users can delete their Gotcha account and associated data. In most cases, you can complete deletion directly inside the app.

In-app deletion (recommended)

Delete your account directly from the Gotcha app:

  1. Open Gotcha
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select Delete Account
  4. Follow the confirmation steps to finalize deletion

If you are unable to access the app, use the email method below.

Email deletion request (alternative)

If you cannot delete your account in the app, you can request deletion by email:

  • Send an email to: review.gotcha@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Include the email address or identifier used to sign in (Kakao / Google / Apple).

We may ask for additional information only to verify account ownership.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we delete the following data associated with your account:

  • Profile information (e.g., profile photo, Instagram ID, campus/university, gender, and other profile settings you provided)
  • Service usage data linked to your account (as applicable)
  • Other content you submitted to the service (as applicable)

Retention period

Account deletion is processed as soon as possible after the request is confirmed. Some data may be retained for a limited period where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or dispute-resolution purposes, after which it will be deleted.

Sign-in methods

Gotcha does not provide username/password login. We support social login only:

  • iOS (App Store): Kakao, Google, Apple
  • Android (Google Play): Kakao, Google
